# IR831 填表指南 + 70A 申请信模板

## IR831 表格字段

| 字段 | 填什么 |
|---|---|
| File No. | 你的税务档案号 (6N1-F...) |
| Year of Assessment | 要修订的课税年度 (如 2024/25) |
| Charge No. | 税单号码 (从评税通知书取) |
| Due Date | 缴税限期 (从评税通知书取) |
| Postal Address | 通讯地址 |
| Daytime Contact Phone No. | 日间联系电话 |

### 勾选项

- **第 2 项**：✓「薪俸入息过多」，填 `1/4/YYYY` to `31/3/YYYY+1` is $______（修订后薪俸总额 = IR56B Total − GSU 内地豁免）
- **第 25 项**：✓「Others」，写：「GSU (share award) income should be apportioned based on geographic source per DIPN 38. The portion attributable to services rendered outside Hong Kong (Mainland China) during the vesting period should be exempt under Section 8(1A)(c) of the IRO. Details in the attached computation.」

### Late Objection 栏（如已超 1 个月）

填：「The delay was due to the taxpayer's unawareness of the entitlement to claim geographic source exemption on GSU income at the time of filing. This was an inadvertent omission, not deliberate. We rely on Section 70A for correction.」

## 70A 申请信模板（英文）

```
To: The Assessor, Salaries Tax
Inland Revenue Department
P.O. Box 28777, Concorde Road Post Office, Kowloon, Hong Kong

Date: [DATE]

Re: Application under Section 70A of the Inland Revenue Ordinance
    Taxpayer: [NAME]
    File No.: [FILE_NO]
    Year of Assessment: [TAX_YEAR]
    Charge No.: [CHARGE_NO]

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hereby apply under Section 70A(1) of the Inland Revenue Ordinance
("IRO") for correction of the above assessment on the ground that
there was an error/omission in my tax return resulting in excessive
assessment.

BACKGROUND

I transferred from Google's Mainland China office to Google (Hong Kong)
Limited on [TRANSFER_DATE]. Prior to this date, I was employed in
Mainland China. Certain GSU (Google Stock Unit) awards granted during
my Mainland China employment continued to vest after my transfer to
Hong Kong.

ERROR/OMISSION

In filing my [TAX_YEAR] individual tax return, I reported the GSU
income at the full amount as shown on the Employer's Return (IR56B)
of HK$[IR56B_GSU], without claiming an exemption for the portion
attributable to services rendered outside Hong Kong during the
vesting period.

CORRECT POSITION

Under Section 8(1A)(c) of the IRO and DIPN 38, the GSU income should
be apportioned based on the ratio of Hong Kong service days to total
vesting days (grant date to vest date). The portion attributable to
Mainland China service is exempt from Hong Kong salaries tax.

Based on the attached computation:
- GSU total income (per IR56B): HK$[IR56B_GSU]
- Hong Kong source (taxable):   HK$[HK_SOURCE]
- Mainland China source (exempt): HK$[CN_EXEMPT]

The assessable income should be reduced by HK$[CN_EXEMPT], from
HK$[CURRENT_ASSESSED] to HK$[REVISED_ASSESSED].

SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS

1. Morgan Stanley "Your Alphabet Stock Statement" showing grant dates,
   vest dates, and prorated income for each GSU lot
2. IR56B for the year of assessment [TAX_YEAR]
3. Employment contract / transfer letter confirming [TRANSFER_DATE]
   as the start date of Hong Kong employment
4. Detailed computation schedule (attached)

I would be grateful if you would review and revise the assessment
accordingly. Please do not hesitate to contact me at [PHONE] if you
require any further information.

Yours faithfully,

[NAME]
HKID: [HKID]
```

## 需附文件清单

1. ✅ Morgan Stanley Stock Statement (CSV 或 PDF，含 grant/vest dates + prorated income)
2. ✅ IR56B（该年度）
3. ✅ 入职证明 / employment contract / transfer letter（确认转移日期）
4. ✅ 本核算报告（HTML 打印版 或 PDF）
5. 可选：Vialto 计算表（如有，作为前年度对比参考）
